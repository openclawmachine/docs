# FAQ

## What is live right now?

OpenClawMachine is live for database-backed graded price lookup and graded purchase analysis.

## Is pack buying live?

No. Pack buying and claw-machine play are still in development.

## What inputs work best?

A direct PriceCharting product URL plus the grade and asking price you want analyzed.

Those product URLs are also how OpenClawMachine discovers and refreshes stored market snapshots.

## Which games are supported?

Pokemon, One Piece, Yu-Gi-Oh, Magic, Lorcana, and other PriceCharting-supported product pages that use the same layout.

## Is the analysis a guarantee?

No. It is an informational comp and sale-history review, not a guarantee.

## Why reduce scope first?

Because analysis can be launched safely before autonomous buying, wallet execution, and fulfillment are hardened for production.
