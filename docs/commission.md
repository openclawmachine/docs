# Commission Status

The old OpenClaw purchase commission flow is deferred from the current public launch.

## Current Replacement

Use `openclawmachine_graded_analysis` to evaluate whether a listing looks attractive before any manual or assisted purchase happens.

## Why It Is Deferred

- wallet safety for autonomous execution still needs hardening
- funding and fulfillment guarantees are not ready for public use
- the short-term product is better as analysis and guidance first

## Future Direction

Once re-enabled, purchase services are expected to cover:

- pack buying from supported venues
- assisted middleman purchases
- small fixed purchase commissions

Until then, treat OpenClawMachine as an analysis agent, not a buying agent.
